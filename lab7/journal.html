Playing with the Dogs

12:00PM August 2nd 2023

Today I played tug of war with the dogs. Try as they could, I would win. I won't admit however that they only lost because they couldn't get a firm stance due to the marble floor. I also played ball with them and took them out for a walk. They were a little fiesty on the walk, probably because they wanted to jump on every person they saw and I had to restrain them back. To top it off, when I was going to bed, they would continue to jump on me, and get me to play with them.

Getting Soaked

3:00PM June 23rd 2022

Today we went on the Cave of the Winds tourist attraction at Niagra Falls. This is basically as close as you can get to the Falls as possible. As such, I was drenched from head to toe. I didn't mind it because the scorching hot summer sun dried me up very quickly and just as soon as I left, I was already yearning to go back and get drenched again.

Hibernation

10:00PM December 27th 2023

Today was a pretty rough day. Outside was very cold, no snow however so I had no reason to go out. Inside, staying in bed all day was so difficult. I could really feel the stress building up as I sipped on some hot cocoa in my heated room watching Netflix all day. I think I should treat myself tonight, that is by trying to break my record for the most amount of hours slept(My record is only 12 hours).
